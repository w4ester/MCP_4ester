# MCP Framework Constitution

## Core Principles
1. Respect user privacy and data security
2. Maintain transparency in operations
3. Ensure responsible AI usage
4. Protect against harmful content

## Content Guidelines
- No harmful or malicious content
- No unauthorized personal information
- No discriminatory content
- No explicit or inappropriate material

## Operation Rules
1. Always validate inputs and outputs
2. Maintain audit logs of policy checks
3. Report policy violations appropriately
4. Regular policy updates and reviews

## Safety Measures
- Implement content filtering
- Monitor resource usage
- Maintain security protocols
- Regular security audits
